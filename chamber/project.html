TechFlow - Site Plan

Your guide to the latest in technology

1. Site Name

TechFlow

The name "TechFlow" was chosen because it represents the continuous stream of technology news, updates, and innovations. It suggests a smooth, ongoing delivery of tech-related content that keeps users informed and engaged with the latest developments in the technology world.

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of TechFlow is to provide:

  • Up-to-date technology news and articles
  • In-depth product reviews and comparisons
  • How-to guides and tutorials for various technologies
  • A community platform for tech enthusiasts to share insights

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1: As a small business owner looking to upgrade my company's technology infrastructure, I need to find reliable information about the latest business software and hardware options to make informed purchasing decisions.

Scenario 2: As a college student studying computer science, I want to stay updated on emerging programming languages and development tools to enhance my skills and stay competitive in the job market.

4. Color Schema

The following color palette will be used throughout the TechFlow website:

Blue #2E5077
Gray #F0F0F0
Orange #FF6B35
